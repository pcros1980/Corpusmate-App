About CorpusMate

CorpusMate lets you search eight real-world corpora — academic, news, spoken, games, and more — and get AI-powered linguistic commentary on what you find.

The eight corpora

BAWE — British Academic Written English
Undergraduate and postgraduate academic writing across disciplines.
Elsevier — Science Research Corpus
Published scientific journal articles across STEM fields.
BASE — British Academic Spoken English
Transcripts of university lectures and seminars.
TED Talks
Transcripts of TED conference presentations.
BBC Teach
Educational content from BBC Teach — accessible journalistic register.
Movie Subtitles
Film and TV subtitle text — informal, spontaneous spoken language.
Games — Video Games Corpus
Video game scripts and interactive dialogue.
Simple English Wikipedia
Plain-language encyclopaedic entries.
